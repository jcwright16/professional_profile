Personal Background

My name is Jason Wright and I currently work as an Industry and Function Decision Science Practitioner for Accenture. I live in Indianapolis with my wife, Liz.

I enjoy coding and solving technical problems, and I especially enjoy applying coding solutions to recreational problems. Some examples of what I refer to as recreational problems are in the Projects page of this site, like building a custom web application for D&D character sheets, or finding out which party of Pokemon is the best for taking on the Elite Four. I have had a long-standing interest in what I think of as "optimizing fun".

In addition to games, I have a deep love of theme parks and Halloween. I believe that both of these are institutions built around the principles of capturing individuals' imaginations and transporting them to exciting and otherworldly environments. In my quest for developing technical knowledge and skills, I hope to some day be able to deliver visual and interactive experiences that spark joy and create happy memories for those who partake in them.
